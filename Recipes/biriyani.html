Recipe for making biriyani

This is a famous indian dish, filled with spices and aromatics. Known for having fluffy and tasty rice mixed with delicious chicken or many other different kinds of meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  • salt as required
  • 2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 3 green cardamom
  • 2 clove
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 cup hung curd
  • 2 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • water as required
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 600 gm chicken
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon saffron
  • 1 tablespoon bay leaf
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4 green chillies
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger
  • 2 drops kewra
  • 1 tablespoon rose water

Steps to prepare the dish

  1. Prepare saffron-kewra water and chop veggies
  2. Saute the onions
  3. Cook biryani on low heat for 5-6 minutes
  4. Serve hot chicken biryani with your favourite chutney or raita
